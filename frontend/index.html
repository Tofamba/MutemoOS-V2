📖 Physical volumes (ZLR, All England, SALR): Open to the headnote page, photograph clearly in good light, select the correct source and volume year, and upload. OCR reads the citation, judge, subject chains, and summary automatically.

🌐 ZimLII / SAFLII / BAILII: Download the judgment PDF or copy the full text, select the matching source, and upload. The ZimLII URL field lets you link directly back to the original.

⚖ Authority weight is set automatically — Zimbabwe cases are binding, South African cases highly persuasive, English cases persuasive.
